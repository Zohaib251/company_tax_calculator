Company Tax Filing Guide | FBR IRIS Portal

1 Visit IRIS Portal
🔗 Go to: https://iris.fbr.gov.pk
2 Login to IRIS Portal
Enter your NTN and Password
3 Select Return Type
Click on "Income Tax Return" → then Select Tax period 2025
4 Business Assets / Equity / Liability
On left sidebar click on Business then Business Assets / Equity / Liability. Then enter the value on respective fields.
Code Description What to Enter
3351 Authorized Capital Enter the total authorized capital of your company as per registration documents
3351 Issued, Subscribed & Paid up Capital Enter the total paid-up capital of your company as per SECP records
✅ After entering values, click "Calculate"
5 Tax Computation
On left sidebar click on Tax Chargeable then Computations. Then enter the value on respective fields.
Code Description Total Amount
923173 Accounting Profit / Tax Chargeable u/s 113C @17% 0
✅ After entering values, click "Calculate"
6 Attach Final Accounts & Statements
On top bar of form, click on Attachment option.
Section What to Upload
Final Accounts (3000) Upload your company's final accounts file (PDF/Excel)
Manufacturing & Trading / Profit & Loss (9230) Upload your manufacturing & trading / profit & loss account statements file (PDF/Excel)
✅ After uploading files, click "Save"
7 Attributes Configuration
On top bar of form Click on Attribute option.
Field What to Select
Resident Status Select Resident if company is Pakistani based, or Non-Resident if foreign based
Business Sector-1 Select Services / Service Provider - Information Technology for IT companies
✅ After selecting, click "Save"
8 Company Director Information
On top bar of form click Company Director option.
⚠️ Important: You need to enter company all Directors information.
Field What to Enter
CNIC Enter the CNIC number of the director (e.g., 12345-6789012-3)
Name Enter the full name of the director
% in Capital (SECP) Enter the percentage of capital the director holds as per SECP records
Capital Amount Held Enter the actual capital amount the director holds in the company
⚠️ Important: After entering director information, click "Add" then click "Save"

20 Balance Sheet (Assets = Equity + Liabilities)
On left sidebar click on Business then Business Assets / Equity / Liabilities section. Enter the values based on your company's financial position.

📌 ASSETS

Enter the value for each asset field in the portal. If you don't have a particular asset, leave it blank or enter 0.

  • Land (Code: 3301) — Enter the value of land owned by the company
  • Building (Code: 3302) — Enter the value of all buildings owned
  • Plant / Machinery / Equipment / Furniture (Code: 3303) — Enter the value of all plant, machinery, equipment, and furniture
  • Motor Vehicle (Code: 3304) — Enter the value of all motor vehicles owned
  • Intangible (Code: 3305) — Enter the value of intangible assets (goodwill, patents, trademarks, etc.)
  • Pre-Commencement Expenditure (Code: 3306) — Enter the value of expenses incurred before business commencement
  • Capital Work in Progress (Code: 3308) — Enter the value of capital work in progress
  • Long Term Investments (Code: 3311) — Enter the value of long-term investments
  • Advances / Deposits / Prepayments (Code: 3312) — Enter the value of advances, deposits, and prepayments
  • Trade Debtors / Receivables (Code: 3313) — Enter the value of trade debtors and receivables
  • Inventories (Code: 3314) — Enter the value of inventories
  • Stocks / Stores / Spares (Code: 3315) — Enter the value of stocks, stores, and spares
  • Short Term Investments (Code: 3316) — Enter the value of short-term investments
  • Short Term Advances / Deposits / Prepayments (Code: 3317) — Enter the value of short-term advances, deposits, and prepayments
  • Current Portion of Long Term Investments (Code: 3318) — Enter the current portion of long-term investments
  • Cash / Cash Equivalents (Code: 3319) — Enter the value of cash and cash equivalents
  • Other Assets (Code: 3348) — Enter the value of any other assets not listed above

✅ Total Assets (Code: 3349) will be auto-calculated by the portal.

📌 EQUITY

Enter the value for each equity field in the portal.If you don't have a particular equity, leave it blank or enter 0

  • Authorized Capital (Code: 3351) — Enter the authorized capital of the company
  • Issued, Subscribed & Paid up capital (Code: 3352) — Enter the paid-up capital
  • Share Deposit Money (Code: 3353) — Enter the share deposit money
  • Capital Reserves (Code: 3361) — Enter the value of capital reserves
  • Revenue Reserves (Code: 3362) — Enter the value of revenue reserves
  • Funds (Code: 3363) — Enter the value of funds
  • Accumulated Profits (Code: 3364) — Enter the total accumulated profit from all previous years
  • Revaluation Surplus (Code: 3365) — Enter the revaluation surplus

📌 LIABILITIES

Enter the value for each liability field in the portal. If you don't have a particular liability, leave it blank or enter 0

  • Long Term Borrowings / Debt / Loan (Code: 3371) — Enter the value of long-term borrowings and loans
  • Liabilities against Assets subject to Finance Lease (Code: 3372) — Enter liabilities against finance lease assets
  • Deferred Liabilities (Code: 3373) — Enter the value of deferred liabilities
  • Provisions / Contingencies (Code: 3374) — Enter the value of provisions and contingencies
  • Short Term Borrowings / Debt / Loan (Code: 3381) — Enter the value of short-term borrowings and loans
  • Current Portion of Long Term Liabilities (Code: 3382) — Enter the current portion of long-term liabilities
  • Advances / Deposits / Accrued Expenses (Code: 3383) — Enter the value of advances, deposits, and accrued expenses
  • Trade Creditors / Payables (Code: 3384) — Enter the value of trade creditors and payables
  • Other Liabilities (Code: 3398) — Enter the value of any other liabilities not listed above

✅ Total Equity / Liabilities (Code: 3399) will be auto-calculated by the portal.

⚠️ IMPORTANT BALANCING RULE

Total Assets (3349) must equal Total Equity / Liabilities (3399)

✅ After entering all values, click "Calculate" to auto-balance.
